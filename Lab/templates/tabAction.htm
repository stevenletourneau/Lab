        **Important Information for Students**

        Course Availability

        All courses in which you are currently enrolled; should appear in your "My Courses" list. Courses may show as 'unavailable' for these reasons:

        1) The instructor has not yet made the course available to students
        2) The instructor is not using Blackboard
        3) The course start date has not yet been reached
        4) The course end date (if specified by the instructor) has passed
